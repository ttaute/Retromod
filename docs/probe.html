Will this mod transform?

Drop a Minecraft mod .jar and Probe checks whether Retromod is likely to translate it onto a newer version: what loader and version it is built for, which removed or renamed APIs it touches, and whether anything is a known wall. It is a pre-flight screen, not a guarantee.

Drop a mod .jar here, or click to choose
Fabric, NeoForge, or Forge. Nothing is uploaded.

100% in your browser. The jar is unzipped and analyzed locally using the decompression built into your browser. It never leaves your machine: there is no upload and no server.

The mod list comes from Modrinth's public API. The version you pick is fetched straight from Modrinth's CDN to your browser and analyzed locally: it is not sent to any Retromod server.

Reports are only a screen. See how mods really ran in the Compatibility DB, or the mods that cannot be translated.
